Power vs Panel Tilt

What students should notice

  • Power is highest when the sun rays are closest to perpendicular to the panel surface.
  • Changing panel tilt changes the incidence angle and therefore the usable irradiance.
  • Even with the same panel and same sunlight, poor alignment reduces power output.

Teaching idea

Ask students to move the sun elevation and predict the new best tilt angle before checking the graph. Then compare winter-like low sun angles with midday high sun angles.
