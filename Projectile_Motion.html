What students should notice

  • Horizontal motion stays uniform when air resistance is ignored.
  • Vertical motion is accelerated downward by gravity.
  • Changing the angle changes both range and maximum height.
  • Different planets produce different trajectories because gravity changes.

A useful classroom activity is to compare the same launch on Earth, Moon, and Mars, then ask students why the paths are different.
