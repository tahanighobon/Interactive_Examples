What students should notice

  • Linear functions form straight lines and have the same rate of change everywhere.
  • Nonlinear functions curve, bend, turn, or grow at changing rates.
  • Changing coefficients can stretch, shift, reflect, or reshape the graph.

A good teaching activity is to ask students to predict the graph shape first, then move the sliders to test their prediction.
