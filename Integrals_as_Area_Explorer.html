What students should notice

  • As the number of subintervals increases, the approximation becomes more accurate.
  • Different numerical methods give different approximations for the same interval.
  • Area below the x-axis subtracts from the total integral.

Teaching idea

Let students predict whether the integral is positive, negative, or near zero before checking the value. Then change the function and interval to compare the visual area with the numerical answer.
