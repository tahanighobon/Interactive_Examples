What students should notice

  • A larger spring constant means a stiffer spring and less extension.
  • Series systems are softer than either individual spring.
  • Parallel systems are stiffer because the springs share the load together.
  • The force-extension graph is linear for an ideal Hooke’s law spring.

A good classroom activity is to keep the mass the same and switch between single, series, and parallel systems to compare the extension and oscillation period.
