What students should notice

  • Larger glazing areas usually increase cooling demand strongly because of solar gain.
  • Occupancy increases cooling load and can reduce heating load due to internal gains.
  • Climate has a major influence on both cooling and heating demand.
  • Room size affects envelope area, ventilation volume, and internal load magnitude.
