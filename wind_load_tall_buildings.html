What students should notice

  • Taller buildings tend to experience larger lateral displacement under the same wind speed.
  • The upper zone usually sees higher wind pressure than the lower zone.
  • Smoother or more aerodynamic shapes can reduce pressure and sway effects.
  • As sway increases, stiffness systems such as cores, outriggers, or bracing become more important.
