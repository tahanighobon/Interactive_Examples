Key formulas

Turnaround Time = Completion Time − Arrival Time
CPU Utilization = (Busy Time ÷ Total Time) × 100%

What students should notice

  • FCFS is simple, but long jobs can make later jobs wait a lot.
  • SJF often reduces average waiting time by running shorter jobs first.
  • Round Robin improves fairness by giving each process a time slice.
  • Idle time affects CPU utilization, especially when no process has arrived yet.
