What students should notice

  • The same panel can produce different power depending on tilt alignment.
  • The best tilt changes when the sun elevation changes.
  • Poor alignment lowers output even if irradiance is strong.

Teaching idea

Ask students to set a low morning sun angle and then a high noon sun angle. Let them compare how the best panel tilt changes in each case.
